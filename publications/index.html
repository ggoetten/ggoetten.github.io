Curriculum Vitae   lattes

My research is focused on biomaterials, development and improvement of these devices. My initial research was on improvement of the natural oxide layer of titanium implants via plasma electrolyte oxidation. In my Ph.D. I start working with hydrogels and these are the two main materials I am mostly working in these recent research years.
